Gesture Lab

Keep your hands in frame to pinch, pan, or frame your highlight while watching the canvas react in real time.

  1. Once camera access is granted, the live preview appears automatically next to the canvas.
  2. Hold your hand 1-2 feet away and keep the center of your palm visible.
  3. Pinch to scrub the timeline or hold an open palm to highlight a particular day.
